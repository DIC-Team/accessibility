Know Our Team

About team

Poornima Subramanian, CEO and Founder of the DIC (Digital Inclusion Compliance) is a digital accessibility coach, leader, evangelist in the Information Technology digital space since 2006. She had traveled through countless journeys of working with diverse industry clients and organizations to both coach and lead the digital accessibility inclusion efforts into the software/product development life cycle. She says.

“This new aspect of starting a company focused on digital accessibility makes me feel proud and excited to broaden the digital accessibility awareness and implementations for all types of organizations from small scale to large scale, one-time audit to placing a subject matter expert, web to mobile app, waterfall to agile, UX to development, Training to making experts.”

Who We Are

We are an emerging and thriving team of digital accessibility professionals who are subject matter experts, developers, testers and program leaders.

"One size does not fit all, especially in digital accessibility space. Using the automation tools to run accessibility scans can be a great start, however it is essential building reasoning aspects, understand the spectrum of digital user needs and start shaping the digital assets (website, mobile app, etc.) that can be accessed by everyone. And we are here to bring that kind of a structured approach irrespective of your needs that can be just a one-time audit or hiring us for end-to-end long term audits - by our team of experts"
