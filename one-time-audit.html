One-Time Auditing

One-time auditing == One-and-done and a best “Super Saver” service option. Golden rule: Short-and-One-term contract hiring one or more of our experts

How it works?

This involves 3-step process.

  • Business requirement analysis*
  • Engage our experts with your business or product development to perform a comprehensive (thorough) audit for your digital product**
  • Defect validations/project closure***

* Our expert will do an analysis of your business requirement and recommend if one-time auditing can be a best solution for your needs.

** The auditing will happen for the current live or fully ready redesigned website/mobile app. There will be no agile sprints/redesigns can be introduced during the audit

***Project closure can be included only if the business has shorter timeline (<4 weeks) as a goal to address all the defects identified during the audit
